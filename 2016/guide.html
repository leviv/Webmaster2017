Phantom 4

Battery

The new DJI Phantom 4 is a mid-range consumer drone, and cen be flown for up to 28 minutes, taking high quality videos. However, the drone takes approximately 1 hour to charge.

Camera

The Phantom 4 features an updated camera that can take very high quality video. The camera can capture up to 4k resolution and 60 frames per second, which is great for slo-motion videos.

Other Features

The Phantom 4 comes complete with a number of special features that sets it apart from the competition. It has front facing obstacle avoidance, which prevents the drone from crashing. It has Active Visual Subject Tracking Technology, which allows it to track a single subject. Also, the drone features quick release propellors, which allows for quick storage.

DJI Inspire

Battery

The DJI Inspire can have up to 18 minutes of fly time, and only takes 22 minutes to charge. This allows for fast charging after the battery is depleted.

Camera

This drone is one of the highest end consumer drones, and has interchangable lenses, which can record up to 4k video, at 60 fps. However, the color and quality of the video is superior something such as a Phantom 4, and can aproach almost movie-like quality.

Other Features

The Inspire can fly at a top speed, and has optical flow stabilization, which allows for super-smooth shots. In addition, the drones transforming helps keep the rotors from interfering in shots.

Syma X5C

Battery

The Syma X5C has an underwhelming battery, because you can only fly for 7 minutes after 100 minutes of charging.

Camera

The camera on the Syma X5C has a max resolution of 720p, and a max frame-rate of 30fps. The uninterupted flight allows for very smooth shots, but the resolution is quite resticted.

Other Features

This drone features 360 degree aversion, which means can it can avoid objects in any direction. The camera also has a 6-axis gyroscope, which prevents the camera from shaking when in flight. It also has elastic blade guards, which protects the rotors.

TBS Vendetta

Battery

The Vendetta has a very similar flight time to the the Syma X5C, but can charge within a smaller time frame.

Camera

The Vendetta is primarily a racing drone, and because of this, the camera is a very small sensor compared to other similarly priced drones. The camera can record up to 480p at 30fps

Other Features

The TBS Vendetta is one of the few commercial drones whose body is nearly completely customizable, even having an option for carbon fiber. Because of this, repairs and upgrades can be done very easily on the drone.
