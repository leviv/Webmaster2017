About


The overwhelming majority of this website was hand-coded from scratch by Levi Villarreal. The languages used were HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Jquery, coded on the text editor Brackets, by Adobe . The website is hosted for free through GitHub, which also provided the domain. A few plugins were used throughout the website, such as the script for the bird movement in the history section, but each of them are open source on GitHub and are listed under the plugins section. Most of the images used are from Pixabay, which has a CC0 Public Domain license, which means that the pictures are free for commercial use and no attribution is required. All images on the TSA side of the website were taken and used with the permission of chapter members. Furthermore, most of the videos used throughout the website are either embedded YouTube videos or free media clips which are also attributed in the video section. All motion elements and graphic designs were made by either Rachel Ussery or Levi Villarreal using Adobe Illustrator. All text was written by either Jillian Taylor, Yolanda Thomas or Levi Villarreal, with the research properly cited under the research section.
